03
The Studio

ABOUT
CODEFORGE

A boutique software studio built on the belief that great software changes everything.

HJB CodeForge is a boutique software development studio founded by Henning Botha — a full-stack developer with a passion for building things that work beautifully and perform powerfully.

We started CodeForge to serve the clients who are most often underserved by the industry: small businesses and non-technical founders who have great ideas but need a trusted technical partner to bring them to life.

The phoenix in our logo isn't accidental. It represents what we believe about software and business — that with the right foundation, anything can be rebuilt stronger, faster, and better than before.

Every project we take on gets our full attention. We don't churn out templates. We don't disappear after launch. We build powerful software, precisely — and we stand behind everything we ship.

Precision Over Speed

We take the time to build it right. A fast, broken product is worth less than a solid one that took an extra week.

Radical Transparency

You'll always know where your project stands. No surprises, no jargon — clear communication at every step.

Client Empowerment

We build software you understand and can grow with. Handover documentation and training are part of every engagement.

Craft Matters

Clean code, thoughtful architecture, and interfaces people enjoy using. The details are where the real work happens.
