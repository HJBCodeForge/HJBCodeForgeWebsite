// Fitness First

A REAL TRAINING ENGINE
UNDER THE FANTASY.

Strip away the lore and VoidRise is a serious, evidence-based hypertrophy & strength app. The dark fantasy is the motivation layer — the training underneath is the real thing, built on the same principles a good coach would use.

See it for yourself →
01

Progressive overload, autoregulated

Double-progression plus an end-of-set RIR check ("how many more reps did you have?") tunes every next session's load and volume to your real performance — not a generic template.

02

Enforced 48–72h recovery

The split engine respects muscle protein synthesis windows so you never smash the same group before it's recovered. The recovery heatmap shows exactly what's primed and what's still healing.

03

A 4-part scientific session

Every workout is structured: Primer (dynamic warm-up) → Heavy Hitters (compounds) → Accessories (isolation volume) → The Shield (stabilizers & injury prevention).

04

Smart splits for your week

2 days → Full Body · 3 → Push/Pull/Legs · 4 → Upper/Lower · 5 → a hybrid. The Smart Split Matrix matches proven training frequencies to the days you can actually show up.
